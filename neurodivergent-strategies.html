How it works

This isn't a personality test. It's a parts map — designed specifically for neurodivergent brains — that shows you exactly which internal parts are standing between where you are and where you want to be.

01
Answer 36 questions
Each question is written around a specific moment where something you value is within reach — and something blocks it. Rate how likely each one sounds like you.
02
Get your top 3 parts
Your answers are scored across 12 Neuro Spicy Parts. Your top 3 — the ones most active in your system right now — are revealed with a full description.
03
Get actionable tips
Each result comes with 3 ADHD-friendly strategies for working with that part — not fighting it — so you can start moving toward your goals this week.
